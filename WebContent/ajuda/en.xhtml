Challenges

The challenges module proposes a way to register challenges (problems or doubts) related to the metabolic network of the organism in question in the system. The module provides four main operations: registration, removal, editing and full view of the challenge information.
The data for the “challenge” object are:
Id: Automatically generated by the system, it represents the unique identifier in the system and in the database; Title: Challenge title, this field will appear as information in other modules, to represent the challenge; Project: Indicates the specialty, that is, the group of specialists who will have access to the challenge; Status: Field that indicates if the challenge is open, or if it has already been solved. When the challenge is created, the system automatically puts its status open. Owner: This field represents the researcher who will be responsible for the challenge, who will perform tasks to validate and maintain the challenge; Description: In this field, the challenge author will describe the problem or doubt proposed in the challenge;

2.1 List of registered challenges

On the home page, after login, or through the "Menu> Challenges> Challenge's Search" the user has the table of challenges previously registered (in the challenge registration module) according to that user's specialty.

The table allows the search and filtering of challenges. To do this, simply enter the desired search in the Search field. The search does not differentiate between upper and lower case letters, that is, it is not case sensitive.

Register challenges

To register a new challenge, in the top menu access "Challenges" then "New challenge".

The "New Challenge" screen will be displayed.

After registration, e-mails will be sent to the person in charge and the team registered with the same specialty as the challenge.

Edit challenge

To edit information on a challenge already registered, click on the “Edit” button. Cada desafio, da lista, tem os seus correspondentes botões de operações. The same registration form window will open, but with the selected challenge data already filled out.

Delete challenges

To remove a registered challenge, just click on the "Delete" button corresponds to the same line. A confirmation window will appear. Just confirm!

On the screen containing the list of challenges, not all existing data is displayed. To view all information, just click on the hyperlink of the challenge name.

You will be directed to an informational page containing all the challenge information, including the answers and the more detailed challenge resolution options in the section below.

3 Challenge Resolution

This module was developed to help researchers to create their hypotheses of answers to solve the challenges.

3.1 Answer challenge

This task consists of creating answer (which will be analyzed and validated by the person in charge) and future solutions for each registered challenge. At the end of the detailed challenge page, the user describes the title of his answer, a field that will represent the answer. And the description, that is, your justification in relation to the change in the metabolic network, after filling in the fields, just click on the "Answer challenge" button.

A successful registration message will be displayed, and the answer will be included in the response board listing for the selected challenge.

3.2 Answer board of the selected challenge

On the detailed challenge page, below the challenge description, the table with all registered responses for the challenge selected in the challenge list is displayed. The response table used has the same structure as the challenge table, that is, it will create pages if there are more than ten (10) answer registered. Navigation is also done through the "Previous" and "Next" buttons.

In the answer board, the following answer information is displayed:
Id: Unique identifier of the response in the system; Pesquisador: Responsible for creating and maintaining the response; Title: Title registered by the user; Description: justification regarding the change in the metabolic network; Justification for Validation: If the answer has been validated by the challenge owner, the justification for the validation is displayed in green; Comment: Reports the number of comments on this answer;

3.3 Delete answer

In the answer board itself, the user who created the answer can perform an exclusion. Each registered answer in the list has its "Delete" button , to delete just click on the button corresponding to the answer. A confirmation window, with “Id” and “Title”, will be displayed. Just confirm!

3.4 Answer validation

After analyzing all responses and their comments, the challenge owner can choose the best answer and validate, that is, as an attainable solution to the challenge. To validate an answer, the user responsible for the challenge simply clicks the "Validate" button for the best answer.

Then a field will appear to fill in the reason for validation and final confirmation. After processing the system, the chosen answer will appear with the green icon next to it.. Only one answer can be validated, so, when there is already a validation, it will be deselected to give rise to a new mark.

4 Mailing list

The discourse list was implemented with the idea of enabling the group of the challenge's specialty, making comments linked to the challenge responses. Each researcher registered in the group can register, edit and delete comments. To view the comments for each answer, simply click on the "Comments" hyperlink in the answer line. To the left of the comment hyperlink the number of comments to answer is informed

The comment will be linked to the researcher who made it, being the responsibility of the same, the edition or deletion. In the comment itself, you can perform the editing and deletion tasks, described in the following topics.

4.1 Post comment

To register a comment, on the challenge response screen, the user must click on the "Comment" button of the answer.

A comment window will appear, at the bottom of the window there will be a field for writing the comment:

Just type in the comment and click the "Comment" button. After saving the database, the system will show a message indicating success in the operation.

4.2 Edit comment

To edit a comment, the user must find it in the panel's comment list, and click on the "Edit" button.
The comment text to be edited will appear in the text box:

After editing the comment, just save by clicking the "Edit" button.

4.3 Delete comment

To delete a comment, just click on the "Delete" button of the desired comment, the system will delete the record from the database.
